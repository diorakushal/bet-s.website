Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

Privacy and security are very important to us at BETS ("BETS," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy") explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our platform (the "Platform"), which includes our website and mobile app. BETS provides a community for sports fans to share their betting activity, follow friends, and engage socially. Please read this Policy carefully.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

When you register for BETS, create an account, or contact us, you may be required to provide certain personal information ("Personal Information"), such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Username and profile information
  • Other optional data like interests or preferences

We do not allow registration via social media accounts (e.g., Facebook, Google).

Transaction Information

When you share or log bets on the Platform, we may collect details about those bets, including:

  • Date and time
  • Wager details (type of bet, odds, event, amount staked)
  • Associated sportsbook (if linked)

BETS does not process or handle payments and does not facilitate betting transactions. Instead, you may be routed to licensed sportsbooks in your jurisdiction.

Information Collected Automatically

We and our partners may automatically collect certain data when you use the Platform, such as:

  • IP address, device ID, and browser type
  • Pages or features you access before, during, and after using BETS
  • Location information (to comply with state betting laws and fraud prevention)
  • Cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies

You can adjust your browser/device settings to block cookies, though some features may not work properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide access to the Platform and features
  • Verify age and eligibility
  • Respond to questions and provide customer support
  • Send authentication codes (SMS/email)
  • Personalize your BETS experience
  • Improve and develop the Platform
  • Prevent fraud, enforce policies, and comply with legal obligations
  • Provide you with tailored content, promotions, or advertising

Note: Transaction information you post is by default visible to other members of BETS.

Sharing of Information

We may share information:

  • With trusted service providers who support our operations (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • With affiliates and partners, but only as permitted by law and your choices
  • In aggregated or de-identified form that does not identify you personally
  • In connection with business transfers (e.g., merger, acquisition)
  • As required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security

We do not sell your Personal Information to unaffiliated third parties for marketing without your opt-in consent.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete certain Personal Information in your account settings
  • Opt out of marketing emails by using the "unsubscribe" link
  • Delete your account at any time by contacting us

Some information, such as posted betting activity, may remain visible if shared before deletion.

Data Retention

We retain Personal Information as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee protection against unauthorized access.

Legal Age Requirement

BETS is not directed to individuals under the legal betting age in their jurisdiction (commonly 21 in the U.S.). By registering, you confirm that you meet the minimum age requirement.

External Websites

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., sportsbooks). BETS is not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review their policies when visiting.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new effective date. By continuing to use BETS after changes, you agree to the updated terms.
